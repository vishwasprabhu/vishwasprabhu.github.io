Intro

I am an experienced Data Scientist specializing in machine learning, natural language processing and data analytics. Skilled in developing and implementing advanced algorithms to solve complex business problems. Proficient in leveraging tools such as Python, Scikit-learn, Pyspark, and Databricks to build scalable and efficient data solutions. Strong background in protein ML/design pipelines, affinity maturation of antibodies, and prediction of antibody-antigen binding. Demonstrated expertise in developing lead-picking strategies, optimization techniques, and structure-based deep learning tools. Successful track record of delivering actionable insights in IIOT applications focusing on anomaly detection, batch-wise analytics, and ETL pipelines. I have a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of San Francisco and a Bachelor's degree in engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Karnataka (NITK Surathkal). I have worked in companies such as Reliance Industries Ltd (India's largest MNC) and ACG group.
